The Metaverse is a collective virtual shared space, created by the convergence of virtually enhanced physical reality and physically persistent virtual spaces. It represents a future where digital and physical lives are blended, offering immersive experiences through virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), and blockchain technologies.

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) to enable smart, responsive environments
